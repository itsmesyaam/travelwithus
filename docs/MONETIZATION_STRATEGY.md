# Monetization Strategy — KeralaX AI SaaS Platform

This document details the business model, pricing frameworks, partner integration paths, and B2B SaaS revenue models for the KeralaX platform.

---

## 1. Multi-Tiered Subscription Plans

To scale the platform sustainably, we define three main customer tiers:

### 1. Explorer (Free)
- **Features**: Unlimited AI itinerary generations, access to the Explore board, and up to 3 saved plans.
- **Price**: $0.

### 2. Nomad Pro ($9 / Month or $79 / Year)
- **Features**: Unlimited saved plans, interactive PostGIS route mapping view, PDF itinerary downloads, custom packing checklist templates, and priority offline app syncing.
- **Target**: High-intent solo travelers and couples.

### 3. Business Merchant (SaaS Tier — $49 / Month or $450 / Year)
- **Features**: Local tour operator or resort profile indexing, basic click-through analytics, eligibility to bid for sponsored inclusions inside generated plans, and lead tracking.
- **Target**: Homestays, resorts, boutique hotels, and guides.

---

## 2. Partner Program & B2B Integration

### 1. Hotel & Resort Partnerships
- **Sponsored Inclusions**: Registered hotel partners are displayed inside AI-generated itineraries matching the user's budget and region.
- **Bidding System**: Partners set maximum daily bids for impressions. The AI scheduler matches high-bidding partners that satisfy user constraints.
- **Revenue Model**: Pay-per-impression or pay-per-click models.

### 2. Guide & Experience Marketplace
- **Tour Booking Commissions**: Travelers can purchase tours and guide services directly inside the itinerary details panel.
- **Revenue Model**: KeralaX charges a **12% transaction marketplace fee** on all tour bookings processed through the platform.

### 3. Travel Agency API Licensing
- **Model**: Enterprise SaaS licensing.
- **Use Case**: Outbound travel agencies or airlines query the KeralaX route planning API to generate itineraries for clients.
- **Pricing**: Tiered API usage pricing (e.g. $0.05 per API query call above 1,000 requests/month).

---

## 3. Advertising & Content Policy

To maintain a premium, Apple-inspired user experience:
- **No Banner Ads**: Google AdSense or standard banner ads are strictly prohibited.
- **Contextual Sponsors Only**: Promotional slots are displayed as native, organic suggestions (e.g. "Stay at Munnar Tea Hills Resort") rather than blinking pop-ups.
- **Vetted Operators**: Partners must be certified by the Kerala Tourism Department prior to listing.
